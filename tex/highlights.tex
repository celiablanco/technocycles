\Large\textbf{Highlights}
\end{center}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, labelsep=1em, itemsep=0.5em]
\item Collapse--recovery dynamics are simulated across ten 1000-year civilizational futures.
\item Duty cycles (fraction of time a civilization remains active) range from 0.38 to 1.00.
\item Resource depletion rate and recovery fraction are generally the most impactful resilience levers.
\item An effective detectability duration reformulates the Drake Equation's longevity term.
\item Low duty cycles help explain the apparent absence of extraterrestrial signals.
\end{itemize}
